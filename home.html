Introduction

Ever wanted to easily calculate your tip totals from a hard night's work of waiting tables? Have no fear! This app will do the thinking for you. Simply click on "New Meal" above and enter your current meal's base price, tax rate, and tip percentage. Boom! You are done. You will notice the customer charges are updated below. To view a running total of your earnings, click on "My Earnings". Here you can see your tip total, how many meals you served, and the average tip per meal. Wasn't that easy?
